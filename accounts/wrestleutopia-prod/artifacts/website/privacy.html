Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-30 (MVP demo)

WrestleUtopia is an MVP demo site. The current version stores your submissions in your own browser via localStorage for demonstration purposes only. No external servers receive your data in this demo build.

Data We Process

  • Talent profiles you create (name, ring name, city, styles, reel link, etc.).
  • Tryouts you post (organization, city, date, requirements).
  • Applications you submit to tryouts.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to simulate a database. Clearing your cache will erase demo data.

Your Rights

In a production release, you would have rights to access, export, and delete your data. This demo is for product validation only.
